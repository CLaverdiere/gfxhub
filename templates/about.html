What?

gfxhub is a place to share graphics.

Why?

I was working on a raytracing program, and found I had collected several pictures that I thought people would like to see. Instead of hosting them all on some site like Imgur, I decided to make a simple site in Flask for fun.

Possible goals for the site:

  • Rating / Scoring of graphic quality, and promoting popular graphics on the front page.
  • Place to provide information on different techniques used in users' graphics.
  • Promote enthusiasm for computer graphics.
